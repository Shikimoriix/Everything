## Contributing to the Anime & Manga Site Index

Thank you for helping us improve this index! Here’s how you can contribute.

## 🌐 Ways to Contribute

### 1. Add a Missing Site or App
Know a site that fits our [requirements](#-site-requirements) but isn’t listed yet?  
Open a **GitHub Issue** using the “Site Suggestion” template (if available) and include:

- Site name and URL
- Category it belongs to (e.g., Streaming, Manga, Music)
- Why you think it should be added
- Any relevant notes (unique features, ad situation, etc.)

### 2. Correct Existing Information
If something is outdated or wrong, open an issue and tell us:

- Which entry is affected
- What needs to be corrected
- The correct information (with a source if possible)

### 3. Report a Dead or Problematic Site
Found a broken link or a site that has turned malicious? Please report it immediately. We will verify and update or remove the listing accordingly.

### 4. Share Your Opinion
We welcome subjective feedback on rankings and reviews. Different perspectives make the index better for everyone. Just keep discussions civil and constructive.

## ✅ Site Requirements

Before submitting, make sure the site meets these **all** of these criteria:

- **Relevance** – Must be appropriate for its category and related to Otaku culture.
- **Operational** – Not permanently down or inaccessible.
- **Content** – Has enough material to be genuinely useful.
- **Integrity** – Not designed to deceive or harm users.
- **Language** – Usable by an English-speaking audience.

> We review sites regardless of the owner’s preference. If you want a site removed, you must ensure it no longer meets the requirements above (e.g., by taking it offline).

## 📏 Ranking Guidelines

Rankings are assigned per category and take the following factors into account (ordered by importance):

1. Popularity & track record
2. Size of the content library
3. Service reliability
4. Content quality
5. User interface & usability
6. Features & options
7. Community interaction
8. Ad cleanliness (bad ads are noted, but only slightly affect rank)

## 🔄 Grouped Entries

We may group multiple URLs under a single entry when they:

- Share the same backend / content.
- Are alternate domains or redirects to the same page.
- Are extremely similar, low-effort sites that don’t need separate reviews.

## 🚫 What We Don’t Include

Sites that:
- Do not meet the requirements above.
- Are likely to be abandoned shortly after submission.
- Would require excessively high maintenance (e.g., spammy sites needing constant updates).

> We don’t exclude a site just because we personally wouldn’t use it – we review, we don’t recommend.

## 📬 How to Submit

All contributions go through **GitHub Issues** (or Pull Requests if you’re editing data files directly):

1. **Check existing issues** – someone may have already reported the same thing.
2. **Open a new issue** with a clear title and the details mentioned above.
3. **Wait for review** – all submissions are read by maintainers. Updates appear in the repository changelog once processed.

## 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Community Guidelines

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Back up disputes with evidence.
- Understand that reviews are subjective – disagreement is fine, but keep conversations polite.

## 📄 License

By contributing, you agree that your suggestions and feedback become part of this community-maintained index and may be used publicly. A formal contributor license agreement is not currently in place.
